About the New Student Welcome Hub

The AIU New Student Welcome Hub is designed to guide you through every step of your first week: arrival, registration, room allocation, key and card collection, and the WOW (Week of Welcome) program.

Arrival Journey Overview

Short video: from airport/bus arrival until you receive your room key.

AIU Orientation Theme & Values

This year's orientation theme is "Growing Together".

  • Respect: Caring for students from all backgrounds.
  • Support: Helping you adjust to campus life smoothly.
  • Growth: Encouraging personal and academic success.

Orientation Week Milestones

Day Milestone Description Status
Day 1 Arrival & Transport Pickup from airport/bus station and arrival at AIU campus. Completed
Day 2 Registration & Student Card Document verification and student ID card printing. In Progress
Day 3 Room Allocation & Key Delivery Receive your room assignment and key at Residential Lobby. Upcoming
Day 4–7 WOW – Week of Welcome Campus tours, info sessions, and social activities. Upcoming
